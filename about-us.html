Our Story

OBD RDX was founded by a group of passionate vehicle enthusiasts who found a gap in the industry for an automotive scan app for the everyday driver. Our mission is to provide all drivers with a sense of security through a simple yet robust vehicle diagnostics app and scanner tool.

After watching our friends and family members struggle to find a simple tool to get them the answers they wanted about issues with their vehicles, our founders teamed up to develop a solution built for every drivers on the go.

No more feelings of panic or uselessness when your vehicle throws a check engine light—use OBD RDX’s ScanCar app and scan tool.
